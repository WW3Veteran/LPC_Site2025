Projects

Mojave Sphinx

2024 - 2025

LPC's first major project, a liquid propellant rocket that utilized nitrous and ethanol, generating approximately 250 lbf of maximum thrust. Remote fueling was possible by using a servo actuated plumbing system. Culiminated in a successful hotfire test in April.

Jet Engine

2025 - Present

As part of a competition sponsored by the US Air Force, we hope to modify an existing jet engine to maximize thrust and fuel efficiency, while minimizing waste heat. Currently looking into an afterburner design and a Stirling Cycle mechanism.

Hopper

2025 - Present

A continuation of the Mojave Sphinx, we seek to use controlled thrust via microcontrollers to steer the rocket into designated areas along a given pathway.
